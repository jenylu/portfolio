Hi, I'm Jennifer!


I'm an illustrator/writer based in the Bay Area. My main art tool is Procreate but I also love working with goauche, collage, and copic markers. I love drawing memories from childhood, portraits, and comics about fancy pigeons. I am a member of SCBWI as well as a student at Storyteller Academy. When I'm not drawing, I'm coding or rollerskating. I am currently looking for representation.
